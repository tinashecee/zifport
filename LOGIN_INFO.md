# ZIFA Portal - Login Information

## How to Login

1. **Click the "Login" button** in the header navigation (top right)
2. **Enter your email and password** in the login modal
3. Click **"Login"** to access your dashboard

## Account Types Available

### 1. **User** (`user`)
- Basic access to view player database
- Can register players, coaches, and referees
- Access to personal dashboard
- View player statistics and availability

### 2. **Club Administrator** (`club`)
- All User privileges
- Extended club management features
- Can manage multiple club registrations
- Enhanced reporting capabilities

### 3. **Administrator** (`admin`)
- Full system access
- All features available
- Can manage all users and registrations
- System configuration access

## Registering a New Account

If you want to create a new account:

1. Click **"Login"** in the header
2. Click **"Need an account? Register here"** at the bottom of the login modal
3. Fill in:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Select your Role (User, Club Administrator, or Administrator)
4. Click **"Register"**

After registration, you'll be automatically logged in and redirected to the dashboard.

## Features Available After Login

Once logged in, you can access:

- **Dashboard** - View statistics and quick actions
- **Player Database** - Search and filter players
- **Registration Portal** - Register new players, coaches, or referees
- **Player Profiles** - View detailed player information and stats
- **User Account** - See your account information in the header

## Notes

- All data is stored locally in your browser (LocalStorage)
- Demo accounts are created automatically on first load
- You can create additional accounts through registration
- Logout is available in the header menu when logged in
